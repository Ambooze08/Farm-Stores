    BRIEF ABOUT John Deere 5310 TRACTOR IN INDIA

    One of the John Deere tractor brand's most well-known models among farmers is John Deere 5310 tractor. It is one of the most well-liked products available in today's market and is equipped with modern features that help make it the most desired one. John Deere tractors are known for their reliability and lower service costs.

    John Deere tractor 5310 is one of the popular offerings in the full-sized tractor segment. It is well defined due to its advanced features and attachment capabilities. John Deere 5310 price in India ranges from Rs. 10.52 Lakhs to Rs. 12.50 lakhs and can be used for all agricultural and commercial needs. John Deere 5310 4wd is widely used in activities like tilling, sowing, puddling, and hauling and is a fuel-efficient tractor.

    John Deere 5310 Tractor Specifications

    John Deere 5310 is designed with classic elements to appeal to the users and give it a tough and rugged look. Also, the engine is engineered with modern parts to deliver maximum efficiency and the latest features. 

    • The powerhouse consists of a 2900 cc diesel engine unit producing a total output of 55 HP. 

    • John Deere 5310 55 hp engine is rated at an engine RPM of 2400. 

    • The powertrain is attached to the transmission through a dual-clutch. 

    • A collar shift type transmission is offered with this engine. 

    • This model has a 12-speed gearbox with 9 forward gears and 3 reverse gears to deliver optimum performance. 

    • This combination of gears helps to achieve a maximum top speed of 24.5 Kmph in reverse gears and 31.9 kmph in forwarding gears. 

    • To control this machine, an equally effective arrangement is offered in the braking system with oil-immersed brakes. 

    • This tractor is used mainly on farms and consists of a 2-wheel drive and 4-wheel drive setup. 

    • To achieve better control, it is equipped with power steering. 

    • The engine has 3 cylinders and a 2900 CC size. 

    • John Deere 5310 2wd tractor motors are known for their trustworthiness and heavy-duty credentials. 

    • To enhance the performance, this tractor has a 16.9 X 28 / 13.6 X 28-inch rear tyre structure and a 6.5 X 20-inch tyre design in the front. 

    • It is also equipped with a 6 Spline type PTO with an HP of 47.  

    • John Deere tractor 5310 4wd is a full-sized tractor with a wheelbase of 2050 mm and an overall length of 3525 mm. 

    • The ground clearance is 435 mm and weighs 2110 kg. 

    • The model is fitted with modern segments for the convenience of the farmers and operators. 

    • These features possess adjustable seats, strong headlamps, and many more refined parts.

    HP of John Deere 5310 and a combination of features make this model the most efficient in use and value for money in India.

    John Deere 5310 Exclusive Features

    A power steering option helps to increase comfort and productivity in the fields and is also available with a toolbox and hard-top cabin to protect the operator from sunlight. Exclusive features of this segment also include oil-immersed brakes and dual-clutch. This unique feature, paired with a rigid design, makes this John Deere 5310 model a sole and value-for-money choice for farmers.
